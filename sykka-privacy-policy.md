# Privacy Policy for Sykka

Welcome to **Sykka**, your personal AI financial assistant. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your data when you use our mobile application and related services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email, phone number.
- **Financial preferences:** Product comparisons, choices, or financial goals.
- **Applications:** Data shared when applying for financial products (credit cards, loans, mortgages).
- **Support:** Information you provide when contacting support.

### b. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device data:** IP address, device type, OS, and app version.
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, actions taken, and app interaction metrics.
- **Analytics:** Tools such as Firebase or PostHog to improve performance and usability.

### c. Optional Integrations
Future updates may include AECB credit score or bank integrations, used only with your explicit consent.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
- Provide personalized financial comparisons and AI insights.
- Process financial product applications.
- Improve app performance and reliability.
- Communicate with you about updates or offers.
- Comply with UAE data protection laws and financial regulations.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **never sell** your data.  
We only share limited information with:
- **Partner banks and financial institutions** for product applications.
- **Service providers** for hosting, analytics, and security.
- **Authorities** if required by UAE law or for fraud prevention.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security
- Data is encrypted and stored securely in UAE or approved data centers.
- HTTPS/TLS protects all communications.
- Only authorized personnel can perform multiple chats.

---

## 5. Your Rights
You can:
- Request a copy of your personal data.
- Update or correct your details.
- Delete your account or withdraw consent at any time.
- Request permanent deletion via mobile app.

---

## 6. Cookies and Analytics
Sykka may use cookies or equivalent technologies for analytics and personalization.  
You can disable them in your device settings, but some features may not function correctly.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy
Sykka is not intended for individuals under **18 years old**, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

---

## 8. International Data Transfers
If your data is transferred outside the UAE, it will remain protected under equivalent standards.

---

## 9. Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Revised versions will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
